Survey

Overview

Surveying the students was one of the best parts, as students shared their more personal views, such as favorite celebrities, social media influencers, songs, traditions, and how they felt about how they are treated in the school. Knowing this can support the CCA in knowing how to better serve the students.

 

When I was designing this survey, I kept in mind that some students have no wish to go over any details of their process of settling into the US, so this survey remains on the cultural side and not on any political themes.

 

Ultimately, it was enriching to see through the lesser-viewed lenses of African, Middle Eastern, and Southeast Asian cultures through the students’ responses.

 

I hope that you can find an interesting new TV Show or a new song to add to your playlist from this! I myself am looking forward to trying some of the suggested dishes.
