A Cultural Guide on the Origin Countries of Refugee and Immigrant Students at CCA

Project Overview

As there is no definite guide for the cultures and countries of refugee and immigrant students at CCA, this guide’s aim is to raise awareness on important aspects of their cultures, such as beliefs and norms, popular foods, basic phrases in their languages, the political situation in their origin countries and push factors, and more. 

 

With greater awareness of the students’ cultures, the CCA community could become a more comfortable, loving, and safe space for students with all backgrounds. 

 

No matter how different we may look and the language barriers that exist, cultural awareness and connections transcend them. 

 

This project helped me educate myself on the backgrounds of these countries and their current situations. Through this, I learned bits of the conversational language from their languages and even went to restaurants to try their delicious cuisines. It also helped me develop deeper connections with students from these countries at my own high school. 

 

These are the most common origin countries of these students in CCA, but more countries will be added over time. Do not hesitate to email me with any questions, comments, or feedback on the guide. email me!
